Why Choose Pasadena Ponies?

🏆

Championship Legacy

Two SCEYFL-AAU Super Bowl titles and national recognition. Our 6U team secured runner-up in the 2021 AAU Youth National Championships.

🛡️

Safety First

Every coach undergoes rigorous training to teach proper football fundamentals. We prioritize safe tackling and blocking techniques above all else.

🌟

Character Building

Beyond the field, we shape character, instill discipline, integrity, teamwork, and accountability in every young athlete.

A Tradition of Excellence

Since 1987, the Pasadena Ponies have been a cornerstone of youth development in Northwest Pasadena and Altadena. We've mentored countless young athletes who've gone on to excel both on and off the field.

Our alumni include NFL players like Tyron Brackenridge and Calen Bullock, who credit the Ponies for teaching them respect, discipline, and how to overcome adversity.

In 2023, we proudly reclaimed our historic name, Pasadena Ponies, reconnecting with a legacy deeply cherished by our community.
